The Problem

Today's advanced construction management software is built for executives sitting in offices — not for optimizing the workflows of superintendents, foremen, and PMs actually running the job.

📋

Paper Workflows Everywhere

Daily logs, PTPs, T&M tickets filled out by hand, photographed, texted to PMs, then manually entered into spreadsheets. Hours lost every single day.

💸

Paying for Bloat You Don't Use

Enterprise platforms charge millions per year for hundreds of features you may never actually use. Specialty contractors end up paying for complexity they never touch.

🔗

Fragmented & Disconnected

Photos in one app, forms in another, drawings emailed as attachments. No single source of truth. Finding a specific T&M ticket means digging through stacks.

📱

Not Built for the Field

Desktop-first interfaces crammed onto a phone screen. Slow load times, tiny buttons, no offline capability. The field is an afterthought.

The Solution

A platform shaped around your operation

DirtView starts with a powerful core — then we customize it to match exactly how your team works. No wasted features. No forced workflows.

1

Field-First Mobile Experience

Every screen is designed for a foreman wearing gloves on a jobsite. Large touch targets, fast load times, camera-first workflows. Submit a daily log in under 2 minutes. Plus everything can be done offline!

2

Flexible Forms System

Digitize any paper form your team uses — daily logs, PTPs, safety inspections, T&M tickets. Drag-and-drop builder with 20+ field types. No developer needed.

3

Intelligent Drawing Management

Upload drawing sets, auto-detect sheet names via OCR, and get tappable hyperlinked callouts. Pin RFIs directly to drawing locations. Annotation layer for async collaboration.

4

RFIs, Submittals & Change Orders

Structured workflows with multi-party routing, distribution groups, and branded PDF exports. Everything tracked, searchable, and linked to drawings.

5

Your Brand, Your Subdomain

Every tenant gets a branded experience — yourcompany.dirtview.com — with your logo, colors, and custom forms. It feels like your software.
