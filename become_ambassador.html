FAQ

Common Questions

Do I need sales experience? +
No. You're not selling anything. You're making introductions to people you already know. DirtView handles all demos, proposals, and closing.
Is there a minimum number of referrals? +
No. Refer one company a year or ten a month — it's entirely up to you. There are no quotas or minimum activity requirements.
How long does it take to get paid? +
Commission is paid within 30 calendar days of the client's payment to DirtView. You'll receive a commission statement with each payout.
Can I refer my own company? +
Self-referrals are not eligible for commission. The program is designed for introducing DirtView to other companies in your network.
Is this program open to anyone? +
Applications are reviewed individually. We're looking for experienced professionals with genuine industry relationships — not mass enrollment.
What if I'm not sure someone is a good fit? +
Submit the referral anyway. We'll evaluate fit on our end and let you know within 5 business days. There's no penalty for submitting a referral that doesn't convert.
